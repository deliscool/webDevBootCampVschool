What can you plant?

Plants have a particular pH preference, so it is important to know the pH level of your soil.

Here is a list of of plants with their pH preference.

Vegetables
pH preference
  • Beans 6.0 - 7.5
  • Broccoli 6.0 - 7.0
  • Cucumber 5.5 - 7.0
  • Potato 4.5 - 7.0

Flowers/Shrubs
pH preference
  • Azelea 4.5 - 6.0
  • Clematis 5.5 - 7.0
  • Fuschia 5.5 - 6.5
  • Rose 6.0 - 7.0

Fruit
pH preference
  • Blackberry 5.0 - 6.0
  • Goose Berry 5.0 - 6.0
  • Currant 6.0 - 8.0
  • Strawberry 6.0 - 7.5
